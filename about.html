ABOUT BRITISH BETTY

WHO IS BETTY? WHAT IS SHE?

My name is Betty. I am British. I am an old lady. I recently retired and decided to learn computer programming for fun.

Also, most programming seems to be done by young men and that means there is almost nothing 'out there' designed by and for grannies. I guess it is up to us grannies to get up to speed and start making our own IT resources.

My long-term ambition is to be a proper programmer. My short-term ambition is just to have fun. So far, I have really enjoyed this new hobby. It is all very simple and logical, once you sit down and start typing, and much easier than following knitting patterns, which I was never any good at.
